Automated Student Bus Attendence System

SmartRide is a web-based student bus attendance system that replaces manual roll-calls with barcode scanning of student ID cards. It automatically records morning boarding and compares it with evening scans to detect if any student who boarded in the morning did not return, ensuring accurate and efficient tracking without manual effort.
